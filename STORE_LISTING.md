## Long Description

**Understand what you're agreeing to in seconds.**

TermCheck is your personal AI legal assistant that reads the fine print so you don't have to. Using advanced AI models (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google Gemini, and more), it scans Terms of Service, Privacy Policies, and User Agreements to identify potential risks and red flags.

**Key Features:**

*   **🚩 Instant Risk Detection**: Automatically identifies concerning clauses related to Data Sharing, Arbitration, Unilateral Changes, Liability, Jurisdiction, Termination, Intellectual Property, Privacy, Surveillance, and more.
*   **🤖 Multi-Model Support**: Bring your own API key and choose from top-tier AI models like GPT-4o, Claude 3.5 Sonnet, Gemini 1.5 Pro, and many others. Models are fetched dynamically from your provider.
*   **🔄 Dynamic Model Loading**: The extension automatically fetches the latest available models from your selected provider with intelligent caching for optimal performance.
*   **💻 Local AI Support**: Use Ollama to run models completely offline on your own machine - no API key required, 100% private.
*   **🔒 Privacy First**: Your API keys are stored locally on your device using Chrome's secure storage. We never see your keys or your data.
*   **⚡ Smart Analysis**: Intelligently chunks long documents using semantic boundaries (sentences, paragraphs) to ensure accurate, context-aware analysis.
*   **📊 Severity Ratings**: Get clear, easy-to-understand explanations of why a clause is flagged with severity levels (Low, Medium, High, Critical) and color-coded badges.
*   **🔄 Rescan Feature**: Quickly re-analyze the current page to check for updates or test different AI models.
*   **🎯 Sorted Results**: Findings are automatically sorted by severity so you see the most critical issues first.

**How to Use:**

1.  Install TermCheck.
2.  Get an API key from your preferred provider (OpenAI, Anthropic, Groq, Google Gemini, or OpenRouter) - or use Ollama locally.
3.  Enter your API key in the extension settings (skip for Ollama).
4.  Navigate to any legal document page.
5.  Click "Analyze Page" to see a detailed breakdown of potential risks.

**Why TermCheck?**

- All API keys are stored locally in your browser
- No data is sent to TermCheck servers (we don't have any!)
- Analysis happens between your browser and your chosen AI provider
