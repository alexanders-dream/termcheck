# Privacy Policy for TermCheck

**Last Updated:** 21/11/2025

## Introduction
TermCheck ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome extension collects, uses, and discloses information.

## Information We Collect

### 1. User-Provided Information
- **API Keys**: To function, TermCheck requires you to provide your own API keys for third-party AI providers (e.g., OpenAI, Anthropic). These keys are stored **locally** on your device using Chrome's secure storage API. We do not have access to your API keys, and they are never sent to our servers.

### 2. Automatically Collected Information
- **Page Content**: When you explicitly click "Analyze Page", the extension extracts the text content of the current web page (Terms of Service or Privacy Policy).
- **URLs**: The URL of the page you are analyzing is processed to associate the analysis results with the specific site.

## How We Use Your Information
- **Analysis**: The page content and your API key are sent directly from your browser to the AI provider you selected for the sole purpose of analyzing the legal text.

## Your Consent
By using our extension, you consent to our privacy policy.

## Changes to our Privacy Policy
If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on this page.
